Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer farm tours for visitors?

Yes! We offer guided farm tours every Saturday at 10 AM and 2 PM. Tours last approximately 1.5 hours and include visits to our organic vegetable fields, greenhouse, and composting facility. Advanced booking is recommended through our contact form.

Where can I purchase your organic produce?

You can purchase our produce at our farm store (open 7 days a week), at the local farmers market every Sunday, or through our home delivery service. We also offer CSA (Community Supported Agriculture) boxes with seasonal selections delivered weekly.

Do you provide agricultural consultation services?

Yes, we offer professional agricultural consultation for both small-scale and commercial farmers. Our services include soil analysis, crop planning, organic certification guidance, and sustainable farming techniques. Contact us to schedule a consultation.

Are your workshops suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! We offer workshops for all skill levels, from complete beginners to experienced farmers. Our beginner workshops cover basics like soil preparation, planting techniques, and organic pest control. Check our website for upcoming workshop schedules.
